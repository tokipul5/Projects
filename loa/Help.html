Lines of Action

Lines of Action (LOA) is a board game invented by Claude Soucie.

Rules

The object of the game is to bring all of one's checkers together into a contiguous body so that they are connected vertically, horizontally or diagonally

Controlling Play

There are 4 settings to play this game. 1. White Auto and Black Auto 2. White Auto and Black Manual 3. White Manual and Black Auto 4. White Manual and Black Manual
